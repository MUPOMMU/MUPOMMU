MUPO CONCERT 2022

Overview

The MUPO Concert 2021 is initiated by the Multimedia University Philharmonic Orchestra (MUPO) division of the MMUSIC Society. This concert is a great opportunity to bring benefits to the students in MMU and also MMU as a whole by achieving the major objectives. We hope that the following concert would be able to provide the campus with a better exposure to the western musical environment and fundamental knowledge in music. This also provides a channel for the students to express their talents through this event.

The Multimedia University MMUSIC Society MUPO Division, as the organizer, collaborating with Triplet Orchestra, plans to organize the concert with the aim of bringing music and orchestra into the universityâ€™s atmosphere by forming an expanding musical community. This is because we can see in several universities in Kuala Lumpur like Sunway University has established orchestras and concerts that benefits the student and the university itself. The students will get a chance to show their talent and to build up a professional domain in the music field.

In addition, this will expose students, lecturers and staffs of MMU to a different type of world-recognized event. This is because in the past history of MMU, this is the first ever western concert to be held. We believe by having this concert, the MMU community will encounter the spirit of western orchestral music and be involved in the upcoming plans.
