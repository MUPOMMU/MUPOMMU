Introduction of MUPO

Multimedia University Philharmonic Orchestra

Multimedia University Philharmonic Orchestra (MUPO) is a new established division in Multimedia University Melaka Campus. The team which consists of brass, woodwinds, strings and percussion players was officially established in 2019. Throughout the year, the team had successfully organized a few events such as Violin Workshop, Music Trip and more. The team has also participated in events from other faculty as performers. During Movement Control Order (MCO) in March 2020, the team also organized the Stay At Home Collab which encourage musicians from all over the country to participate.
